The five axioms

I · Type is the hero Typography drives hierarchy. Imagery is auxiliary. If removing the imagery breaks the page, you're doing it wrong.

II · Tension is beauty Near-symmetry, not perfect symmetry. Deliberate imbalance. Break the grid at one load-bearing point.

III · Whitespace is pressurized Negative space pushes with intent. It is not what's left over — it is what you PUT there.

IV · Motion has meaning Every animation answers: what problem does this solve? If the answer is "because it looks cool," cut it.

V · Derive, don't decorate Aesthetic DNA is derived per-run from the subject. Not picked from a menu. The subject contains its own design system; your job is to listen.
